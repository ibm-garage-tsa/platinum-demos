<details>

<summary>2 - Process Mining Server</summary>

<br/>

1. Access your Technology Zone environment using the link in the reservation email. You may also use the IBM Technology Zone’s **My Library** → **My Reservations** page.

<br/>

2. If the VMs are not already started, click the **Play** icon to start the **IBM Process Mining Server** virtual machine (VM).

<br/>

3. Click to open the **IBM Process Mining Server** VM. <br/><br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-business-automation-process-and-task-mining/images/vm.png" width="600" /><br/>

4. Press the **ESC** key on your keyboard.

<br/>

5. Click **Not listed?** <br/><br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-business-automation-process-and-task-mining/images/not-listed.png" width="200" /><br/>

6. Enter '**root**' under **Username** (1), and click **Next** (2). <br/><br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-business-automation-process-and-task-mining/images/root.png" width="300" /><br/>

7. Enter '**IBMDem0s!**' under **Password** (1), and click **Sign In** (2). <br/><br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-business-automation-process-and-task-mining/images/password.png" width="300" /><br/>

8. Click **Activities**. <br/><br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-business-automation-process-and-task-mining/images/activities.png" width="200" /><br/>

9. Click **Terminal**. <br/><br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-business-automation-process-and-task-mining/images/terminal.png" width="200" /><br/>

10.	Enter '**cd/opt/processmining/bin**' in the Terminal window.

<br/>

11.	Enter '**./start.sh**' in the Terminal window.

<br/>

12.	Enter '**service nginx start**' when the prompt returns. <br/><br/> You should see the following output: <br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-business-automation-process-and-task-mining/images/output.png" width="400" /><br/>

13.	Wait for several minutes for the Process Mining server to start.

<br/>
