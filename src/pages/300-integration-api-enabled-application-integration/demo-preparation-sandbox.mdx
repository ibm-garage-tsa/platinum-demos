After you are logged in, click <a href="https://cloud.ibm.com/catalog/content/ibm-cp-integration-72f63273-f2f6-4e9c-8626-60fe798c57be-global" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">here</a> to install Cloud Pak for Integration.<br/><br/>Make sure you are in an **ITZ** account (1) and the **Product version** is the latest version available (2).<br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-integration-api-enabled-application-integration/images/prep-image2.2.png" width="800" />

It is important that you choose a password that is 32 characters in length and is composed of only letters and numbers. <summary>3 - Prerequisites, preparing your environment, and delivery options</summary>

<details>

<summary>Set up Salesforce</summary>
<br/>

Now, let’s set up Salesforce. You will need a developer account to use for testing. If you already have a Salesforce developer account, you can use that. If not, you can sign up for a free developer account now.

<br/>

1. Go to <a href="https://developer.salesforce.com" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">Salesforce Developers</a>, and click **Sign Up**.<br/><br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-integration-api-enabled-application-integration/images/salesforce-dev-signup.png" width="800" /><br/>

2. As soon as you have your account, log in with your dev admin account.<br/><br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-integration-api-enabled-application-integration/images/salesforce-dev-home.png" width="800" /><br/>

3. To get your Salesforce integration URL, click your **user profile**. The URL text below your account name is your login URL. Copy and paste it somewhere for later use in this demo.<br/><br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-integration-api-enabled-application-integration/images/salesforce-url.png" width="800" /><br/>

4. Next, you need to retrieve a security token. For this, click your **user profile** and select **Settings** in the profile panel.<br/><br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-integration-api-enabled-application-integration/images/salesforce-settings.png" width="800" /><br/>

5. Under **Settings**, find and click **Reset Security Token**. Then, click **Reset Security Token**, and the newly-generated security token will be emailed to you.<br/><br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-integration-api-enabled-application-integration/images/salesforce-reset-token.png" width="800" /><br/>

6. Next, you will create an Application representing App Connect Enterprise, and retrieve the Consumer Key and Consumer Secret. Click the **setup cogwheel** at the top right.<br/><br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-integration-api-enabled-application-integration/images/salesforce-setup.png" width="800" /><br/>

7. In the navigator on the left-hand side, scroll to **PLATFORM TOOLS**, expand **Apps** (1), and click **App Manager** (2).<br/><br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-integration-api-enabled-application-integration/images/prep-image3.7.png" width="800" /><br/>

8. Click **New Connected App**.<br/><br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-integration-api-enabled-application-integration/images/prep-image3.8.png" width="800" /><br/>

9. Enter **App Connect** (1) as the **Connect App Name** and your email as the **Contact Email** (2). Mark **Enable OAuth Settings** (3).<br/><br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-integration-api-enabled-application-integration/images/prep-image3.9.png" width="800" /><br/>

10. Mark **Enable for Device Flow** (1). Now select **Manage user data via APIs (api)** (2) as the **Selected OAuth Scopes**. Click **Add** (3)<br/><br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-integration-api-enabled-application-integration/images/prep-image3.10.png" width="800" /><br/>

11. Click **Save**.<br/><br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-integration-api-enabled-application-integration/images/prep-image3.11.png" width="800" /><br/>

12. It will take approximately ten minutes for the new connected app to register in Salesforce. Once it does, the **Consumer Key** (1) and **Consumer Secret** (2) display. **Copy** and save the Consumer Key. **Click to reveal**, copy, and save the Consumer Secret, is created (click the link to reveal it).<br/><br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-integration-api-enabled-application-integration/images/prep-image3.12.png" width="800" /><br/>

Your Salesforce dev account is ready.
