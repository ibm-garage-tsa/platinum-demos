<details>

<summary>Set up the demo</summary>
<br/>

1. On the <a href="https://cmc.coc-ibm.com/aiops" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">Watson AIOps cluster page</a>, you will see several clusters. You will be using the **Ezra** cluster. <br/>

<br/> <InlineNotification><strong>NOTE</strong>: Watson AIOps is NOT currently multi-tenant. Therefore, only ONE USER can use the environment at a time. Otherwise, you risk overwriting/undoing other users’ demos. You must reserve your timeslot. The reservations are NOT self-enforcing, meaning it’s up to everyone to adhere to only using the demo during the time you’ve reserved and ensuring you finish up prior to the next person’s time slot.</InlineNotification> <br/>

2.	Reserve the **Ezra** cluster for a self-enablement session by following the steps below: <br/> a. Click **Reservations** to access <a href="https://cmc.coc-ibm.com/calendar/ezra" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">Ezra's Demo Schedule</a>.<br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-watson-aiops-proactive-outage-avoidance/images/ezra-reservations.png" width="300" /><br/>b. To easily view the duration of reservations and avoid interfering with existing reservations, click **week** at the top right to access the weekly calendar view, or you can click individual reservations to see details.<br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-watson-aiops-proactive-outage-avoidance/images/4b.png" width="600" /><br/>c. Click a date of interest in the calendar, and a reservation pop-up window will open.<br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-watson-aiops-proactive-outage-avoidance/images/4c.png" width="600" /><br/>d. To ensure you have sufficient time to set up, practice, and clean up, reserve one to two hours (1). Choose **Self enablement** (2). You don’t need any opportunity or revenue details to use the cluster for self-enablement. Then, click **Reserve Time** (3).<br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-watson-aiops-proactive-outage-avoidance/images/4d.png" width="400" />

<br/>

3. In the <a href="https://cmc.coc-ibm.com/cluster/ezra" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">Ezra cluster details</a> page, go through the list of systems under **Primary Tools/Application UIs** and log in to each of them with the provided credentials. Open a new tab for each system prior to giving the demo. <br/><br/> **Notes**: <br/> • For logging in to the IBM Automation Hub, use OpenShift as the authentication method. <br/> • For Slack, you will use the **#ezra** channel.<br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-watson-aiops-proactive-outage-avoidance/images/primary-tools.png" width="800" />

<br/>

4. On the **Quote of the Day Anomaly Generator** page, click **Reset All Services to Factory Settings**. <br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-watson-aiops-proactive-outage-avoidance/images/reset.png" width="400" />


<br/>

5. Validate that the QotD golden signals in Instana show normal behavior (i.e., the erroneous call rate is 0). <br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-watson-aiops-proactive-outage-avoidance/images/golden-signals.png" width="800" />

<br/>

6. Validate that the reset is complete and that the AI Manager shows no stories and alerts by performing the following steps:<br/><br/>a. On the **AI Manager** page, click **View data and tool connections**. <br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-watson-aiops-proactive-outage-avoidance/images/ai-manager.png" width="800" /><br/>b. Ensure that all connection statuses are **green**. <br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-watson-aiops-proactive-outage-avoidance/images/connections.png" width="800" /><br/>c. Return to the **AI Manager** page and click **View applications**.<br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-watson-aiops-proactive-outage-avoidance/images/ai-manager-home.png" width="800" /><br/>d. Click **Quote of the Day**.<br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-watson-aiops-proactive-outage-avoidance/images/all-applications.png" width="800" /><br/> <InlineNotification><strong>NOTE</strong>: If no stories are available, skip the remaining instructions in step 6 and proceed directly to step 7.</InlineNotification> <br/>e. Expand each story (1) and click **View all alerts** (2). <br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-watson-aiops-proactive-outage-avoidance/images/qotd-stories.png" width="800" /><br/>f. Click the **Filters** icon (1). Under the **State** column, check **Open** (2). Click **Apply filters** (3).<br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-watson-aiops-proactive-outage-avoidance/images/filters.png" width="800" /><br/>g. Select each alert (1) and click **Clear alert** (2). Repeat this until all alerts are cleared. <br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-watson-aiops-proactive-outage-avoidance/images/clear-alert.png" width="800" /><br/> h. Repeat steps 6e-6g for each story until no stories are available.

<br/>

7. On Slack: <br/><br/> a. Validate that you are in the **#ezra** channel. <br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-watson-aiops-proactive-outage-avoidance/images/ezra-slack.png" width="800" /><br/>b. In the chatbox, it is recommended that you type some text to separate old messages and easily identify new messages that will appear while you perform the demo. An example is included in the screenshot below. <br/><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ibm-garage-tsa/platinum-demos/master/src/pages/300-watson-aiops-proactive-outage-avoidance/images/text.png" width="600" /><br/>
